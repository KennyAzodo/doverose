What We Do

OUR SERVICES

From tarmac to terrain — delivering aviation excellence, real-time monitoring, and advanced drone solutions across Nigeria and beyond.

Aviation
Aero Lubricant Supply

We supply a full range of aviation-grade lubricants — engine oils, hydraulic fluids, and greases — sourced from certified manufacturers. Our rapid delivery infrastructure ensures your aircraft are never grounded due to lubricant shortages, keeping turnaround times at a minimum.

Aviation
Aircraft & Cabin Cleaning

Our trained cleaning crews deliver thorough interior and exterior aircraft cleaning to international hygiene standards. From galley deep-cleans to passenger cabin turnovers, we restore every aircraft to a pristine, passenger-ready condition with speed and precision.

Aviation
Lavatory & Water Services

We handle full lavatory waste extraction and potable water replenishment for all aircraft types. Using compliant equipment and strict sanitation protocols, our ground teams ensure every service is completed safely and efficiently between every turnaround.

Aviation
Aircraft Fueling

Precision fueling services for commercial, charter, and private aircraft. Our certified fueling technicians operate in strict compliance with ICAO standards, delivering accurate uplift quantities with rigorous quality checks to ensure flight safety and fuel integrity on every fill.

Aviation
De-Icing Services

We provide professional aircraft de-icing and anti-icing treatments to safeguard operations during adverse weather conditions. Using approved glycol-based fluids and calibrated equipment, our team ensures clean, ice-free surfaces for safe departure every time.

Tracking
Real-Time GPS Monitoring

Our advanced GPS monitoring platform gives you continuous, live visibility of your aircraft, vehicles, and assets. With instant alerts, geo-fencing, and historical playback, you gain total situational awareness — accessible from any device, anywhere, around the clock.

Tracking
Vehicle Tracking Services

Keep your entire ground fleet under control with our real-time vehicle tracking solution. Monitor speed, location, idle times, and driver behaviour through a centralised dashboard — reducing fuel costs, preventing theft, and improving overall fleet discipline and accountability.

Tracking
Fleet Management Solutions

End-to-end fleet management that covers scheduling, maintenance tracking, compliance monitoring, and performance analytics. Our system integrates seamlessly with existing operations to streamline your logistics, extend vehicle lifecycles, and cut operational overhead.

Drone
Agricultural Drone Applications

We deploy precision agricultural drones for crop spraying, field mapping, and yield analysis across large-scale farmland. Our drone pilots optimise coverage patterns for maximum efficiency — reducing chemical usage, boosting crop health, and delivering actionable data to farmers.

Drone
Surveillance Drone Operations

Our surveillance drone teams provide high-resolution aerial monitoring for perimeter security, infrastructure inspections, pipeline surveys, and event oversight. Equipped with thermal and optical sensors, we give security and operations teams a decisive aerial advantage.

Drone
Drone Sales & Maintenance

We supply commercial and industrial-grade drones to organisations across Nigeria, backed by full after-sales support. Our certified technicians handle routine maintenance, component repairs, firmware updates, and pilot training — ensuring your drone fleet stays mission-ready at all times.
